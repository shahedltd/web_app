About Me

Shahed Rahman

Shahed Rahman, a determined and ambitious young talent from Bangladesh, has embarked on a remarkable journey toward becoming a Machine Learning engineer. Born on May 12, 2006, Shahed’s story is one of passion and perseverance. He began his academic journey in the Science department at Satkhira Technical School & College, excelling with a GPA of 4.86 in the SSC exams. Currently a diploma student in Computer Science and Engineering at Satkhira Polytechnic Institute, Shahed is also pursuing his dreams beyond academics. His dedication to programming has led him to excel in Python, NumPy, pandas, scikit-learn, Matplotlib, and FastAPI, along with a growing expertise in Machine Learning. Alongside his studies, he’s honing his professional skills as a Python backend developer at Creative Space Bangladesh, contributing to innovative projects. With aspirations of earning a bachelor’s degree in Germany, Shahed is preparing for a future filled with exciting possibilities, blending his love for technology, learning, and creativity.
